🦉 *Did you know* CODE LEAP has a *Duck rule*? 🦆

*Lock your computer* whenever you step away. Leave it open and a teammate may *duck* you on the team channel, with a small penalty into a fund that turns into *team treats*. A fun nudge toward good security habits.

*How to use it:* press *Ctrl+Cmd+Q* on a Mac to lock before you walk off. From Code Coffee.
